About the Department

The Department of Computer Engineering of Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Polytechnic, Amravati imparts diploma-level technical education as per the curriculum prescribed by the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Mumbai.

The Department is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi.

The department is equipped with well-established computer laboratories and experienced faculty, and regularly conducts Faculty Development Programmes, quizzes, paper presentations, seminars and other academic activities for the benefit of teachers and students.
